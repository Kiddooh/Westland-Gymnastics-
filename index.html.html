About Westlands Gymnastics

Our Story

Westlands Gymnastics was founded in 2010 by Sarah Johnson, a former national team gymnast with a passion for nurturing young talent. What started as a small training facility with just two coaches has grown into Nairobi's premier gymnastics center.

Our mission is to provide a safe, supportive, and challenging environment where gymnasts of all ages and skill levels can develop their abilities, build confidence, and achieve their personal best.

Our Facility

Located in the heart of Westlands, our 10,000 square foot facility features state-of-the-art equipment including:

  • Olympic-grade sprung floors
  • Multiple balance beams of varying heights
  • Uneven bars and parallel bars
  • Vaulting tables and runways
  • Tumble tracks and trampolines
  • Dedicated strength and conditioning area

Safety First

We prioritize safety above all else. All our coaches are certified in first aid and CPR, and our equipment is regularly inspected and maintained to the highest standards. We maintain low student-to-coach ratios to ensure every gymnast receives proper supervision and instruction.
